Introduction

In this survey, we would like to evaluate different image generation methods. Given a text prompt, we would like to see which method produces an image that most closely aligns with it.

We would also like to know whether or not you are red-green colorblind.

Examples

We provide an example for reference.


What to do?

We will show you 2 images from 2 different methods.
You are asked to choose the ONE that you think looks more red-green colorblind friendly.
You will do 45 rounds of selection and it takes about 10 minutes. Many thanks for your help!

For any comments or questions, please email to mhua2@andrew.cmu.edu
